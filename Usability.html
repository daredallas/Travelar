Usability Goals

Effectiveness

Although maps are useful, those without a sense of direction might not be able to recognise locations or directions. The augmented reality travel app allows for quick visual feedback on where to go or what they’re looking at through their perspective.

Efficiency

Automatically grabs geographical data from an internet connection or gps if active and should load relative information quickly when used.

Safety

User’s individual information will not be accessible but aggregated data will be collected and anonymized to improve the services.

Learnability

Easy to use gyroscopic controls that is essentially the same as pointing a camera or looking at an object. Simple tappable prompts for location information.

Criteria

Time to complete a task, time to learn a task, number of errors made.
